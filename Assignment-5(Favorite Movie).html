Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is Rush Hour 2 featuring Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker.
It's a movie that I remember watching when I was young and its comedy-action dynamic
is always really entertaining for me to watch.

Jackie Chan is one of my favorite actors and I love watching his brilliant choreography.
Chris Tucker is a great comedian and is able to make magic on screen when he's present.
Together they make a really great dynamic to forge my favorite trilogy in movies.
